About Us :

FURNITURE-IN – Imported Office Chairs & Tables

FURNITURE-IN is United State’s largest furniture brand. It was established in 1988 and it’s been a brilliant, exciting journey for us since then. Our chairs have a competitive edge in terms of ergonomic & aesthetic value functionality and price.


We love bringing alive your dream space. We emphasize comfort and aesthetics while delivering well designed, fun and functional furniture solutions to you.


FURNITURE-IN keeps a wide range of models made of different materials and for various functionalities. It includes Executive chairs, Desk chairs, Stacking and Drafting chairs.


We offer our customers home and office furniture, along with solutions for education and training institutes.
